What are fancy serial numbers?

+

Fancy serial numbers are banknotes with unusual or visually striking serial number patterns. Collectors pay significant premiums for these notes above face value.

What is a Solid serial number?

All 8 digits identical (e.g. 88888888). Among the most valuable fancy notes, worth $500-$5,000+.

What is a Ladder serial number?

Digits run sequentially (e.g. 12345678 or 87654321). Extremely rare and highly sought after.

What is a Radar note?

Reads the same forwards and backwards (e.g. 12344321). A palindrome banknote.

What is a Repeater banknote?

First half of serial repeats in second half (e.g. 12341234). A popular collectors pattern.

What is a Low Number note?

Serial below 00001000. Historically significant first-run notes that command strong premiums.

Values depend on condition, denomination and current demand. Always verify with recent eBay sold listings for accurate pricing.
