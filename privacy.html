Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

FancySerial.money ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

FancySerial.money is a free tool that runs entirely in your browser. We do not require registration or login to use the checker.

We may collect the following:

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily submit it via our email capture form to receive a free Collector Certificate. This is entirely optional.
  • Usage data — if Google Analytics or a similar analytics tool is active, anonymous data such as page views, browser type, country and referral source may be collected. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Advertising data — if Google AdSense ads are displayed, Google may set cookies to personalise ads. See Google's privacy policy for details.

2. Serial Numbers You Enter

Serial numbers entered into the checker are processed entirely within your browser. They are never transmitted to our servers, stored, logged or shared. We have no record of what serial numbers you check.

3. How We Use Your Information

If you provide your email address, we use it solely to send you the Collector Certificate PDF and occasional relevant updates about FancySerial.money. We do not sell, rent or share your email address with third parties.

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.

4. Email Marketing

We use ConvertKit to manage email subscriptions. ConvertKit stores your email address on their servers. You can review ConvertKit's privacy policy at convertkit.com/privacy.

5. Cookies

FancySerial.money itself does not set cookies. However, third-party services we use — including Google AdSense and Google Fonts — may set cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies.

You can control cookies through your browser settings at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

We may link to or integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • eBay Partner Network — affiliate links to eBay listings. eBay Privacy Notice
  • ConvertKit — email list management. ConvertKit Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — web fonts loaded from Google servers.

7. Children's Privacy

FancySerial.money is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

If you are based in the UK or EU, you have rights under GDPR including the right to access, correct or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@fancyserial.money.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of FancySerial.money after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us at hello@fancyserial.money.

This privacy policy applies to fancyserial.money only. It does not apply to any third-party sites linked from this page.
