Frequently Asked Questions

Are $2 bills rare?
Not really — billions have been printed. But they are uncommon in circulation because people save them rather than spend them.
Is a $2 bill with a fancy serial worth more?
Yes, considerably. Pattern premiums are the same as other denominations.
What is a $2 star note worth?
A $2 star note from a small print run can be worth $10 to $50 or more. Combined with a fancy pattern, values can reach $100-$500.
Where can I find $2 fancy serials?
eBay is the most active marketplace. Search '$2 dollar bill fancy serial' for current listings.
