How Much is a Birthday Note Worth? Date Note Values Explained

📅 March 2026⏱ 4 min read

The birthday note has become one of the fastest-growing collector niches. A dollar bill whose serial spells your exact date of birth is legal tender that is also a deeply personalised keepsake — and a genuinely memorable gift.

The Gift Market vs Collector Market

Birthday notes operate in two parallel markets. The gift market tends to pay more because emotional value drives price. Sellers who market birthday notes on Etsy as well as eBay typically achieve better prices than those listing on eBay alone.

Finding Your Birthday Note

  1. eBay — search your birthday in MMDDYYYY format (e.g. "07141990 dollar bill")
  2. Reddit r/birthdaynotes — an active community buying and trading date notes

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a birthday dollar bill worth?
A dollar bill whose serial matches a specific date typically sells for $15 to $75. Notes matching famous birthdays or historical events can fetch more.
What dates are most valuable?
Independence Day 07041776, millennium dates 01012000, and Christmas 12251900s all command premiums.
How do I find a dollar with my birthday?
Search eBay for your birthday in MMDDYYYY format. Our free checker also detects date notes automatically.
Can I order a birthday note from the bank?
No — you cannot request specific serial numbers. Find them through eBay, r/birthdaynotes on Reddit, or by checking notes at home.
